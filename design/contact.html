Get In Touch: We're Here to Connect

Have questions about our programs? Want to explore partnership opportunities? Ready to get involved? Send us a message and we'll connect you with the right person on our team.

We typically respond within 48-72 hours. For urgent inquiries, please call (512) 555-0123.

Response Timeline

  • General inquiries: 48-72 hours
  • Program applications: 1 week
  • Partnership development: 2 weeks
  • Meeting scheduling: Within 1 week

What Happens Next?

  1. We'll review your message and interests
  2. Connect you with the right team member
  3. Schedule a conversation or send detailed information
  4. Invite you to relevant upcoming events or opportunities
  5. Follow up with concrete next steps based on your interests

Accessibility Support

Need communication accommodations? We offer:

  • Spanish language support
  • Extended response times
  • Phone and video call options
  • In-person meetings
  • Alternative communication formats

Just let us know what would be most helpful for you.

Join Us: Regular Community Events

E4 Live Networking Events

When: First Thursday of each month, 6:00-8:00 PM
What: Participant presentations, mentor networking, community connection
Cost: Free, no registration required

Quarterly Community Showcases

When: End of each program session (March, June, September, December)
What: Professional presentations, family celebration, community appreciation
Duration: 2-3 hours with flexible arrival

Annual Community Celebration

When: Early summer (specific date announced annually)
What: Major community celebration with presentations, awards, food, music
Special Features: Intergenerational community connection

Visit Us: Community Spaces and Programming

Programming Locations

E4 Youth programming occurs at multiple community-accessible locations across Austin, ensuring geographic accessibility and community connection.

Primary Community Hub

Address shared upon program enrollment or event registration

  • Daily programming: Workshops, mentorship meetings, small community events
  • Accessibility: ADA accessible, public transit accessible, parking available
  • Amenities: Creative technology lab, presentation space, family-friendly environment

Partner Organization Locations

Programming also occurs at schools, community centers, cultural organizations, and other partner venues based on programming needs and community accessibility.

Drop-In Opportunities

Monthly Program Information Sessions

When: Second Saturday of each month, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
