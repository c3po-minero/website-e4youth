Get Creative! Where Passion Meets Profession

High school students explore creative careers and build professional skills through hands-on projects, industry mentorship, and real-world portfolio development.

Apply Now Learn More

Creative Skills Meet Professional Development

Get Creative! bridges the gap between high school and career-ready skills, using storytelling and digital media as pathways to professional success.

Program Focus

  • Industry-standard software training
  • Professional portfolio development
  • Career exploration and networking
  • Real client and community projects

Who This Serves

  • Ages: High school students (14-18)
  • Duration: Semester programs, summer intensives
  • Outcomes: Professional portfolios, college/career readiness

Professional Skills & Industry Tools

Technical Skills

  • Adobe Creative Suite mastery
  • Web development basics
  • Video production & editing
  • Digital photography

Professional Skills

  • Client communication
  • Project management
  • Portfolio presentation
  • Interview skills

Career Pathways

  • Creative industry connections
  • Internship opportunities
  • College program alignment
  • Professional mentorship

Ready to Get Creative?

Application Process

  1. Submit online application with portfolio samples
  2. Attend information session or portfolio review
  3. Complete brief interview process
  4. Begin your creative professional journey

Program Details

Fall/Spring: 16-week semester programs
Summer: 8-week intensive
Schedule: After school or Saturday options
Cost: Sliding scale with scholarships available
