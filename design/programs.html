Programs That Grow With You

E4 Youth programs create a connected ecosystem of learning opportunities that meet young people where they are and support their growth over time. From first creative explorations to professional portfolio development, every program builds on the last.

Find Your Program Schedule a Visit

Beyond Standalone Classes: An Ecosystem Approach

Traditional youth programming often treats each age group in isolation, creating disconnected experiences that don't build on each other. E4 Youth designs programs as a connected ecosystem where participants can enter at multiple points, progress naturally between levels, and return to contribute as mentors and leaders.

Multiple Entry Points

Whether you're 8 or 25, there's a place to begin your storytelling journey

Progressive Skill Building

Each program level prepares participants for the next stage of learning and opportunity

Community Connection

Older participants mentor younger ones, creating sustainable learning communities

Real-World Application

Every program connects to authentic opportunities for growth and contribution

Three Core Programs, Endless Possibilities

Foundation Level: Digital Storytelling Program (DSP)

Ages: Elementary and middle school students, families

Duration: 6-8 week sessions, ongoing workshops

Focus:

Creative confidence, basic digital literacy, storytelling fundamentals

Outcomes:

Foundation skills, program readiness, family engagement

Learn More About DSP

Development Level: Get Creative!

Ages: High school students

Duration: Semester-long programs, summer intensives

Focus:

Career exploration, portfolio development, professional skills

Outcomes:

College and career readiness, professional portfolios, mentor networks

Explore Get Creative!

Leadership Level: Heritage & Innovation Pathways (HIP)

Ages: College students and emerging adults

Duration: Year-long commitments, project-based work

Focus:

Advanced projects, community leadership, professional development

Outcomes:

Public-facing work, employment connections, community impact

Discover HIP Opportunities

Shared Experiences That Build Community

While each core program serves specific age groups, E4 Youth creates regular opportunities for participants to connect across programs, building mentorship relationships and strengthening our learning community.

Community Showcases

Monthly events where participants from all programs present their work, celebrate achievements, and learn from each other's experiences.

Intergenerational Workshops

Special sessions where HIP participants facilitate learning for younger students, building leadership skills while sharing knowledge.

Community History Projects

Collaborative research and storytelling projects that connect multiple age groups in documenting and sharing community stories.

Mentor Networks

Structured relationships that connect participants across program levels, creating support systems and leadership opportunities.

Ready to Begin Your Journey?

For Youth & Families

  1. Explore our programs and identify the best fit for your age and interests
  2. Attend a community showcase or information session to learn more
  3. Complete application and participate in brief orientation session
  4. Begin your storytelling journey with a supportive community

For Educators & Organizations

  1. Contact us to discuss bringing E4 Youth programming to your community
  2. Participate in program observation and planning conversations
  3. Collaborate on customized programming that serves your participants
  4. Launch partnership with ongoing support and evaluation

For Community Partners

  1. Explore collaboration opportunities that align with your mission
  2. Meet with staff to discuss partnership models and community impact
  3. Develop agreement that benefits participants and community partners
  4. Implement partnership with regular communication and shared learning
