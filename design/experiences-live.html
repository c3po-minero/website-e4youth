E4 Live: Where Stories Spark Opportunities

E4 Live is a dynamic series of in-person networking events that celebrate creativity, connection, and community. These vibrant gatherings bring together emerging creatives, established professionals, and community partners to share work, build relationships, and explore new possibilities.

Attend Next E4 Live Partner on E4 Live

More Than Networking: Building Creative Community

Traditional networking events often feel transactional—focused on business cards and elevator pitches rather than authentic connection. E4 Live takes a different approach, creating spaces where relationships form naturally around shared creative work and mutual inspiration.

Story-Centered

Every event centers participant work, creating genuine conversation starters and deeper connections

Intergenerational

Young creatives connect directly with established professionals, building mentorship relationships organically

Community-Rooted

Events highlight local culture, venues, and creative ecosystems rather than generic corporate spaces

Action-Oriented

Conversations lead to concrete next steps, whether collaboration, mentorship, or professional opportunities

What to Expect at E4 Live

Pre-Event Connection

Before each event, registered participants receive digital portfolios showcasing featured work and creator profiles. This allows attendees to identify specific people and projects they're excited to learn more about.

Creative Showcases

Each E4 Live features 6-8 emerging creatives presenting their latest work in 3-minute spotlight presentations. These aren't formal talks—they're casual shares that invite questions and collaboration.

Facilitated Connections

Rather than leaving networking to chance, E4 Live includes structured activities that help attendees connect around shared interests, complementary skills, or mutual professional goals.

Professional Speed Mentoring

Established professionals rotate through brief mentoring conversations with emerging creatives, providing targeted advice on specific challenges or career pathway questions.

Community Resource Fair

Local organizations, educational institutions, and creative businesses share information about opportunities, programs, and resources available to attendees.

Collaborative Project Launching

Many E4 Live events conclude with idea-sharing sessions where attendees can pitch collaborative projects, seek creative partners, or identify needed resources.

Diverse Events for Different Creative Interests

Quarterly Community Showcases

Frequency: Four times per year
Focus: Major program milestones and community project completions
Audience: Families, community partners, professional mentors, program participants
Format: Portfolio presentations, interactive demos, panel discussions

Monthly Professional Mixers

Frequency: Monthly during school year
Focus: Career pathway exploration and professional skill development
Audience: High school and college-aged participants, working professionals, educators
Format: Industry focus sessions, skill-building workshops, structured networking

Pop-Up Collaboration Sessions

Frequency: Bi-weekly
Focus: Project-based learning and peer-to-peer skill sharing
Audience: Active program participants across all age levels
Format: Hands-on workshops, peer mentoring, collaborative problem-solving

Community Partner Spotlights

Frequency: Monthly
Focus: Highlighting local organizations, businesses, and cultural institutions
Audience: Community members, local business leaders, cultural organizations
Format: Partner presentations, networking, resource sharing
