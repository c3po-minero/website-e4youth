## Program Overview Section
**Heading:** Real Skills, Real Projects, Real Impact
**Icon:** `fa-briefcase`

Get Creative! recognizes that today's creative industries require both artistic vision and technical proficiency. Our program immerses high school students in authentic project work that builds both creative confidence and professional competency.

**What Makes Get Creative! Unique:**
- **Industry-Standard Tools:** Learn the same software and techniques used by working professionals
- **Real Client Projects:** Work on actual projects for community organizations and local businesses
- **Professional Mentorship:** One-on-one guidance from established professionals in creative fields
- **Portfolio Development:** Graduate with a professional-quality portfolio that opens doors to colleges and careers

**Who This Serves:**
- **Primary Participants:** High school students ages 14-18
- **Creative Interests:** Visual arts, digital media, web design, video production, audio production, graphic design
- **Career Goals:** Students exploring creative careers or seeking to develop creative skills alongside other interests
- **Experience Level:** All levels welcome, from beginners to students with existing creative experience

## FAQ Section
**Heading:** Questions Students and Families Often Ask
**Icon:** `fa-question-circle`

**Q: What if I don't know what creative career I want to pursue?**
A: Perfect! Get Creative! is designed for exploration. You'll experience multiple creative tracks and have extensive conversations with mentors about different career paths.

**Q: Do I need to already know how to use professional software?**
A: Not at all. We teach all necessary software skills from the ground up. What matters is your willingness to learn and commitment to the program.

**Q: Can I participate while playing sports or having other after-school commitments?**
A: We work with students to find scheduling solutions, but the program does require consistent attendance. We recommend discussing your schedule during the application process.

**Q: Will this help me get into art school or design programs?**
A: Yes! Many of our graduates receive scholarships to competitive creative programs, and all graduate with portfolio pieces that strengthen college applications.

**Q: What if I want to pursue a creative career but my parents are hesitant?**
A: We welcome conversations with families about creative career paths and often can connect parents with industry professionals who can share realistic information about creative careers.

**Q: Can I continue with E4 Youth after Get Creative!?**
A: Absolutely! Many graduates continue with our Heritage & Innovation Pathways (HIP) program or return as mentors for new Get Creative! participants.
